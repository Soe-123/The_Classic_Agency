ABOUT

Earl Harvin is a professional musician and lifelong automotive enthusiast equally passionate about music as he is classic cars, and just as likely to be found trackside at Brands Hatch as the backstage of Hollywood Bowl.

Decades of working internationally has fostered relationships with the most prominent names in the entertainment industry, as well as exclusive circles of collector car enthusiasts worldwide.

With access to diverse clientele and unique insights into the market, The Classic Agency has the resources to assist or consult your sale or purchase.
